IHS Stall Stories

About

Stall Stories was founded in spring of 2023 by Juliet Crane. We are a club at Ithaca High School that creates bi-weekly single paged newsletters that are distributed on the inside of bathroom stalls around IHS.

Our mission is to provide entertainment, spread news, and foster community throughout Ithaca High School.

Join

Joining is very simple! Just come to our meetings. Anyone can join at any time.

Meetings are every Wednesday during Universal Lunch in room G208.

Feedback

Stall Stories Club loves receiving your ideas and feedback so that we can make the very best Stall Stories!

Report upcoming events, suggest new sections, jokes, riddles, and more by coming to any meetings or contacting us via contacts listed below.
